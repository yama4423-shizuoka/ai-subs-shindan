    About Each AI Service

    ChatGPT Plus — est. $20/mo

    The all-in-one option powered by GPT-4o. Covers writing, image generation (DALL-E), voice conversations, file analysis, and Custom GPTs in a single subscription. The default first choice if you want one plan that handles everything.

    • Best for: writing, summarizing, brainstorming, image generation, voice
    • Ideal if: your needs span many different tasks

    Claude Pro — est. $20/mo

    Praised for its natural, nuanced writing and a large context window that reads long documents and big codebases in one pass. Well-suited to business and technical writing, in-depth research, and serious coding work.

    • Best for: long-document reading, code generation, writing, research
    • Ideal if: you're a researcher, writer, or engineer working with lengthy material

    Google AI Pro (Gemini) — est. $20/mo

    The standout advantage is deep integration with Gmail, Google Docs, Drive, and Calendar. Also excellent at YouTube video summarization, real-time search-grounded answers, and video generation. A lighter "AI Plus" tier is also available at a lower price.

    • Best for: Google Workspace integration, search-grounded answers, video understanding
    • Ideal if: you live and work inside the Google ecosystem

    GitHub Copilot Pro — est. $10/mo

    Built directly into VS Code and JetBrains IDEs, providing real-time code completions and chat-based review and debugging. The most affordable option among the major services and the clear choice for developers who write code every day.

    • Best for: in-editor code completion, code review, debugging assistance
    • Ideal if: you write code as part of your daily work

    Perplexity Pro — est. $20/mo

    An AI search engine that cites every answer with source URLs. Powered by real-time web data, making it ideal for fact-checking, market research, and staying current on fast-moving topics. The best option for replacing traditional web search with AI.

    • Best for: cited search, real-time web information, fact-checking
    • Ideal if: you're a journalist, analyst, or do heavy information research

    Frequently Asked Questions

    What's the difference between a paid plan and the free version?
    The main differences are usage limits and model quality. Free plans cap the number of requests per day and may restrict access to the most powerful models. Paid plans lift those limits significantly and often unlock extra features such as image generation, voice conversations, and longer context handling.
    Is it worth subscribing to more than one AI service?
    It can be, when your use cases are clearly distinct. A popular combo for developers is Claude Pro for writing and long-document work paired with GitHub Copilot for in-editor code completion. That said, start with one subscription and only add a second once you've consistently hit the free-tier limits.
    Can I cancel my subscription at any time?
    Yes. ChatGPT Plus, Claude Pro, Gemini, and Perplexity Pro all run on monthly billing and can be cancelled at any time. You retain access through the end of the billing period and are not charged for the following month. Check each service's official website for details.
    Which AI is the most capable?
    It depends on the task. Claude tends to lead on long-document reasoning and coding quality. ChatGPT covers the widest range of everyday tasks. Gemini shines when integrated with Google Search and Workspace. This quiz is designed to match you with the AI that fits your specific workflow, not just the highest benchmark score.
    Can I use a personal plan for work or business?
    For light personal use it's often fine, but if you handle business data, consider a team or enterprise plan (e.g. ChatGPT Team/Enterprise, Claude for Work) for stronger data-privacy guarantees. Enterprise plans typically exclude your conversations from model training. See each provider's business pages for details.
